Academic Calendar 

The standard academic calendar consists1 of two semesters and a special term.  Each semester normally includes 13 weeks of instruction and 2 weeks of examinations.  The special term takes place during the Semester 2 vacation period. It comprises of two parts, each of which lasts 6 weeks and is inclusive of an examination period.

The table below presents the basic chronological structure of the two main semesters in any academic year.
