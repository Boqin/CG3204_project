Non-discrimination Statement

It is the policy of the National University of Singapore to comply with all Singapore nondiscrimination laws and the relevant Singapore government policies. This nondiscrimination policy applies to admissions, employment, access to and treatment in the University programs and activities. Complaints of invidious discrimination prohibited by University policy are to be resolved within existing University procedures.
