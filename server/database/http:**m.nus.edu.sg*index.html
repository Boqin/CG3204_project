Not to leave behind the Android community, NUS has recently launched Android applications. With many more application in line check out this space for what are the new application launches for the convenience of all students, staff and visitors of NUS..

Around NUS

Around NUS is updated with 2013 information!! Around NUS allows you to find out about National University of Singapore (NUS) - from what's iconic and what's exciting to what's yummy. This application also comes with detailed maps of NUS' Kent Ridge Campus, University Town, Bukit Timah Campus and Outram Campus, enabled to determine your current and other locations in NUS. You may download the android version from the google play! Download NOW!!

NUS Carparks

NUS Carparks application allows users to get up-to-the-minute carparks availability information within the National University of Singapore (NUS). The application also comes with detailed maps of the carparks locations and the user's current location in NUS. You may download the android version from the google play! Download NOW!!

NUS IVLE

NUS IVLE Android app launched!
Now you can enjoy the services of IVLE while being on the go with your Android smart phone.
NUS IVLE is NUS' course management system running on Android phones. This free app can be used via Wi-Fi and 3G.

You can access to your course modules, syllabus, urgent announcements, read assignments from the work bins, review forum post and see the latest student events from IVLE.

Getting started is easy. Just download the app from Google Play, establish the connection and you are on the way. Do let the IVLE team know about your experience with this app.



NUS NextBus

Nus NextBus application allows the android users to get up-to-the-minute bus schedules and be informed well ahead of the next bus’ arrival time. The application also comes with detailed maps of the bus stop locations and the user’s current location within NUS. You may download the android version from the google play! Download NOW!!

NUS mSurvey

NUS Survey App is now written for Android phones to record survey results and export them to your computer for further analysis.  It does not require Internet Connection while you are conducting the survey interviews.  The results will be stored on the phone until you connect it to your computer via iTunes to upload the results to your computer.  Further analysis can be done on the results in excel.

NUSCast

CIT launches NUScast Android app for mobile access to recorded NUS public events and open course lectures
The NUScast app for Android by the Centre for Instructional Technology gives you access to recorded NUS public events while you are on the go.
By logging in using your NUSNET userid, you can also access NUS open course lectures and your module-related webcasts.
Install the NUScast Android app and give us your feedback.

NUS AlumNet

Office of Alumni Relations has just released the AlumNET app. With the AlumNET iOS app, you can now view posts of upcoming events from the NUS Office of Alumni Relations, Faculties/Schools and Halls as well as Alumni Groups (including overseas chapters and faculty-, hall- and interest-based groups). NUS IVLE Communities

The NUS IVLE Communities iOS app allows you to access IVLE communities on the go. With the app, you
can check urgent announcements, email community members, read and reply forum posts, view files in the workbin and access weblinks added by community members. You can also manage your community membership, or search and join communities that you are interested in.
You can do it all using your NUSNET user ID and password. Getting started is easy. Just download the app, establish the connection and you are on your way. Please let the IVLE team know about your experience using the IVLE Communities app.
This application is developed by Centre for Instructional Technology(CIT) NUS.

NUS Bulletin

With the NUS Bulletin app, you can now read brief synopses of each module offered at NUS while on the go. You can search the NUS Bulletin by keywords or module codes.
This app is free, and it can be used via WiFi and 3G.
Getting started is easy. Just download the app, establish a connection and you are on your way.
This application is developed by Centre for Instructional Technology(CIT) NUS.

NUS IVLE

IVLE iPhone is NUS’ course management system running on an Apple’s iPhone. This app which is free for staff and students can be used via WiFi and 3G. The app makes interacting with IVLE easier by providing access to course modules, syllabus, allows to check urgent announcements, read assignments from the workbins, review a discussion forum post and see the latest student events from IVLE.
This application is developed by Centre for Instructional Technology(CIT) NUS.
