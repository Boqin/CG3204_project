Asia, Australia & New Zealand

The Asia/Australasia (Australia & New Zealand) region offers a wealth of history, adventure and academic excellence.

Ride the Asia wave!  Discover the modern cities of Asia by embarking on an affordable exchange to ASEAN, China, Japan, Korea and India.  Live it up in China and India - the new economic powerhouses - and experience for yourselves the vibrancy and changes happening everyday.  Strengthen your knowledge of Asia and learn from premier teaching institutions like Peking University, University of Tokyo, Seoul National University and Indian Institutes of Technology.

Hungry for challenges and adventures?  Take on Australia and New Zealand and experience adrenalin-pumping activities like white water rafting, sky diving, surfing - your perfect escapades from the hectic and crowded life in Singapore!  Advance your innovation and research by learning from industry experts and even Nobel Laureates at top ranking universities like University of Melbourne and University of Auckland.

Canada, Mexico & United States of America

Experience a journey of opportunity and adventure, serene nature and rich culture when you go on an exchange to the Americas!

Canada – The Great Outdoors

Do Canadians really live in igloos and play ice hockey all year round? Find out for yourself in the Land of Natural Wonders! With its vast winter terrains, lush forests and majestic waterfalls, be enchanted by the beautiful scenery during each of Canada’s four seasons. The dynamic and vibrant cities of Canada also promise to intrigue you. Parlez-vous Francais? Take the chance to learn some French, one of the country’s official languages, and embrace a whole new lifestyle away from home!

Mexico – A Journey through Time

Hola aventureros! Come experience the rich 20,000 year-old culture and heritage of the Aztec and Mayan civilisations in Mexico. Witness the majestic architecture of the ancient Aztec pyramid temples and the artistry of the Mayan hillside terrace farms. In contrast, modern Mexico is a culturally diverse and economically vibrant nation, and the most populous Spanish-speaking country in the world. Pick up Spanish phrases, practise your salsa moves and charm your way into the hearts of the locals!

USA – Embrace Diversity & Adventure

A vibrant country that needs little introduction! But debunk your knowledge of the USA gained through mass media and find out what’s real by going on an exchange to the States! Experience first-hand one of the most versatile systems of higher education in the world for an unforgettable academic and personal experience. Embrace adventure, diversity and infinite possibilities in the Land of Opportunities!

Europe, Middle East & Africa

Europe defies easy categorisation and has so much to offer, with a bewildering array of diversity and culture, cosmopolitan cities and spectacular scenery.

Immerse yourself in its rich artistic heritage of literature, music and fine arts, or marvel at and discover Europe’s wondrous architecture such as the Eiffel Tower and the Sistine Chapel. 

Learn from the best in terms of the sciences, business acumen, humanities, and technological research. You will have the chance to work in the same laboratories that once housed Albert Einstein, or to study in the libraries where Jean-Paul Sartre wrote his famous works of philosophy.

Benefit from the centuries of European academic excellence. Seize the opportunity to study in world-renowned universities that are grounded in years of solid research and development. Not just a tourist destination, Europe boasts outstanding institutions which can create a positive impact on your education, both academically and culturally.

Have the best environment to master languages such as French, German, Dutch, Swedish through daily immersion.
