Bursaries
Students in need of financial aid should first approach the University's Office of Financial Aid, which offers a comprehensive package of financial aid combining work study assistance, study loans and bursaries. We will also be considering students who applied to Office of Financial Aid for our Bursaries.
When you apply for any FASS bursary, we will consider you for every other eligible bursary offered by FASS for that semester.
