Transport Services

The Transport, Logistics and Car Park Department maintains a fleet of vehicles and a pool of drivers to provide transportation services to NUS’ departments. The division also oversees NUS Shuttle Bus Services and acts as the liaison office for NUS when dealing with public transport operators.


This department also manages the appointed outsourced car park contractor to manage car parks services.  The car park contractor carries out enforcement action against any parking violations and reserves parking lots when requested by our customers.  Other responsibilities of the department include provision of logistics support for NUS corporate functions; provision of standard uniforms to staff at various NUS departments; delivery of the NUS internal mails to the various faculties and departments;   and the procurement of term contractors for the supply of standard office supplies and furniture for NUS’ faculties and departments.
